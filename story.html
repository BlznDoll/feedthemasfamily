Our Story

Feed Them As Family was created from love, research, and the belief that dogs deserve better than mystery ingredients and over-processed food. This mission is personal.

Our founder, a passionate dog lover, was frustrated by the lack of transparency and quality in the pet food industry. After extensive research and experimentation, they discovered the benefits of feeding dogs a raw diet. This led to the creation of Feed Them As Family, a company dedicated to providing real and raw dog food that promotes health and happiness for dogs everywhere.

Why Feed Them As Family Exists

In 2010 I rescued Rocky from the SPCA. It didn't take long before he quickly became a part of the family. I loved him so much that I decided to rescue another. So, I brought Rj into the family and they became the best boys I ever had. As they grew older both of my boys started developing health issues.

Rocky was diagnosed with lymphoma cancer in 2019 and I wanted to do everything I could to change his condition. I immediately began doing holistic research and doing holistic practices to help extend his life but I knew there had to be a connection between diet and overall health. This is what got me involved into looking for better food alternatives for pets.

Shortly after, I discovered the biologically appropriate raw food (barf), which is the basis for which my raw dog food is created around. I want to ensure pets get a proper balance of nutrients. With a proper diet I was able to prolong Rocky's life 2 years longer than expected.

Rocky and Rj are my inspiration for creating Feed Them As Family- Raw & Real Dog Food. I am a true believer that with a nutritional diet you can achieve a healthy optimal life for your pet.

Charles Eads

Founder & CEO
