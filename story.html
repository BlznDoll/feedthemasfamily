Our Story

Why Feed Them As Family Exists

Feed Them As Family was created from love, research, and the belief that dogs deserve better than mystery ingredients and over-processed food.

A Personal Mission

Inspired By Rocky And RJ

In 2010, I rescued Rocky from the SPCA. It did not take long before he became part of the family. I loved him so much that I decided to rescue another dog, and RJ joined the family. They became the best boys I ever had.

As Rocky and RJ grew older, both of them started developing health issues. In 2019, Rocky was diagnosed with lymphoma cancer, and I wanted to do everything I could to help him. I immediately began researching holistic practices and better food alternatives because I believed there had to be a connection between diet and overall health.

That search led me to biologically appropriate raw food, also known as BARF. It became the foundation for the way Feed Them As Family approaches raw and real dog food. My goal is to help pets receive a better balance of nutrients through meals made with care.

With a better diet and intentional care, I was able to help prolong Rocky's life two years longer than expected. Rocky and RJ are the reason this business exists.
