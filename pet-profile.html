Pet Profile FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Pet Profile free?

Yes. The Pet Profile helps us understand your dog before recommending meal options.

Can I fill this out for more than one dog?

Yes. If you have multiple dogs, please submit a separate profile for each dog so we can review their needs individually.

Do you help first-time raw feeders?

Yes. We can help you understand meal options and how to begin the raw feeding process safely.

Will this replace my veterinarian’s advice?

No. Our recommendations are for food guidance only. Always speak with your veterinarian about medical conditions, allergies, or major diet changes.
