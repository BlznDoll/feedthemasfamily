Store meals frozen until you are ready to thaw and serve.

Serve Fresh

Product FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Use the contact page to ask about availability, pricing, pickup, delivery, or custom meal options.

Do I need to complete the Pet Profile first?

It is recommended, especially if your dog is new to raw feeding or has allergies, sensitivities, or special needs.

Are meals custom made?

Some meals may be available as standard options, while custom meal recommendations can be made after reviewing your dog’s Pet Profile.

Are these meals complete and balanced?

Product details should clearly state whether each meal is complete and balanced or intended for supplemental/intermittent feeding. Please contact us with questions before ordering.
