Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Feed Them As Family collects, uses, protects, and shares personal information when you visit our website, contact us, submit a pet profile, or purchase our products.

Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Feed Them As Family operates this website to provide information, products, services, customer support, and a curated shopping experience. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you visit our website, use our services, make a purchase, complete a form, or communicate with us.

By using this website, you acknowledge that you have read and understand this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, information collected automatically when you use our website, and information from service providers that help us operate our business.

Information You Provide

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Billing and shipping address
  • Pet profile information
  • Order details
  • Messages or customer support inquiries

Payment and Transaction Information

If you make a purchase, payment information may be collected and processed by our payment processor or ecommerce provider. We do not directly store full credit card numbers on our website.

Automatic Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Website interactions
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use personal information to:

  • Respond to messages and customer inquiries
  • Review pet profile submissions
  • Recommend meals or products
  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Arrange pickup, delivery, or shipping
  • Improve our website, products, and services
  • Send order updates or service-related messages
  • Send promotional emails if you choose to receive them
  • Prevent fraud and protect website security
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Pet Profile Information

If you complete a Pet Profile, we may collect information about your dog, including name, breed, age, weight, current diet, activity level, allergies, sensitivities, and health-related notes.

This information is used only to help us understand your dog’s needs and provide meal guidance. Pet Profile recommendations are not veterinary advice. Please consult your veterinarian before making major dietary changes, especially if your dog has a medical condition.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, remember preferences, and support marketing or advertising features.

You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being used. Some website features may not function properly without cookies.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information for money. We may share personal information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business, including:

  • Website hosting providers
  • Ecommerce platforms
  • Payment processors
  • Email or marketing platforms
  • Shipping, delivery, or fulfillment providers
  • Analytics providers
  • Customer support tools

We may also disclose information if required by law, to respond to legal requests, to protect our rights, or to prevent fraud or security issues.

7. Relationship With Shopify

If Feed Them As Family uses Shopify or another ecommerce platform, information you provide during shopping, checkout, or order processing may be collected and processed by that platform.

Shopify may process personal information according to its own privacy practices. You can review Shopify’s privacy information here: Shopify Privacy Policy .

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party websites, platforms, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, security, or content of third-party websites. Please review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

9. Email Marketing

If you sign up for emails or promotions, we may send you updates, product information, offers, or educational content. You can unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time by using the unsubscribe link in the email or contacting us directly.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

11. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to help protect personal information. However, no website, internet transmission, or electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Please avoid sending sensitive or confidential information through unsecured channels.

12. Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including customer service, order records, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and business operations.

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to:

  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt out of promotional emails

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information listed below. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.
